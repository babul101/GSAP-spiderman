Marvel Spiderman

Spider-Man is one of Marvel Comics' most iconic superheroes. Created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, he first appeared in "Amazing Fantasy" #15 in 1962. Spider-Man's real identity is Peter Parker, a high school student who gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider.
